Company Background

ARTrix was founded by Alanis Trixie H. Rosales in 2020. ARTrix were providing paintings for art enthusiast. Let your mind wander through wild imaginations. Express your emotions in the eye of the beholder lies beauty.

However, truly majestic masterpieces do not always have to be pretty when it comes to artistic expression. Instead, an artwork 's beauty is closely related to its capacity within us to induce emotions. It is the ability of the artist to effectively express a wide range of thoughts and emotions that make art beautiful.
